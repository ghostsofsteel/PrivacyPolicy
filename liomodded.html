Privacy Policy for LioModded!

Last updated: [Date]

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. It only accesses publicly available content on the Lioden website and Lioden wiki to provide enhanced features like marking rarity checks and battle helpers.

Permissions

This extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To access and modify the content of the active tab to provide personalized features.
  • tabs: To detect when you navigate between pages and update features accordingly.
  • notifications: To display alerts and updates relevant to your use of the extension.
  • host permissions (e.g., https://www.lioden.com/*): To read and modify content on specified websites to enhance functionality.

Data Usage

All processing happens locally in your browser. No user data is collected or shared.
